How to Pray the Rosary

Follow the steps below to pray the Rosary.

  1. Make the Sign of the Cross.
  2. Recite the Apostles' Creed.
  3. On the first bead, say an Our Father.
  4. On the next three beads, say a Hail Mary for an increase in faith, hope, and charity.
  5. On the fifth bead, say a Glory Be.
  6. Announce the first mystery and say an Our Father.
  7. On each of the ten Hail Mary beads, say a Hail Mary while meditating on the mystery.
  8. Conclude with a Glory Be and the Fatima Prayer.
